Conceptual Compound Brandable
Canonical Meaning

Conceptual Thesis

The artifact names a mechanism, not a mood: a system stays open to participants while its resources or access rights rotate rather than deplete linearly.

  • Not a literal "revolving door" hospitality or retail reference.
