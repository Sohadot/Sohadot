Domain Acquisition

How to Buy a Domain from Sohadot.com

Secure, straightforward domain acquisition through trusted third-party escrow platforms. Transparent pricing, no hidden fees, and a process built around buyer protection.

1
Browse the Portfolio & Identify Your Domain

Start at the Portfolio page to browse available domains. Use the Valuation Tool to assess market value before reaching out. Every listed domain includes category context, TLD quality, and directional pricing.

2
Send an Inquiry

Contact us via the About page with the domain name you are interested in and your intended use. Include your preferred transaction platform. We respond to all serious inquiries within one business day.

3
Agree on Terms

We agree on a price and transaction platform. All pricing is transparent — no surprise fees added after the fact. Directional valuations from our published framework inform starting discussions.

4
Secure Transaction via Escrow

Payment is handled through your chosen trusted platform — Escrow.com, Afternic, or Sedo. Funds are held in escrow until the domain transfer is verified and complete. We initiate the transfer as soon as escrow confirms payment.

5
Domain Transfer — Complete

After escrow releases funds, the domain is pushed to your registrar account. Most transfers complete within 24–72 hours depending on the registrar and TLD. You receive full transfer confirmation and ownership is entirely yours.

Common Questions
Frequently Asked Questions
Is there a minimum purchase price?
There is no fixed minimum. Prices are set per domain based on quality, category, TLD, and comparable-market signals. Our valuation framework is publicly documented so buyers understand how prices are derived.
Can I negotiate the asking price?
Yes. We are open to serious, market-informed offers. Provide context about your intended use and offer rationale. Offers significantly below published comparable values require justification to be considered.
How long does the transfer take after payment?
Once escrow confirms payment, most transfers complete in 24–72 hours. .com transfers via Afternic Fast Transfer can be near-instant. International TLDs may take slightly longer depending on registry rules.
What if the domain I want is not listed?
Contact us anyway — we may have unlisted inventory or can advise on acquisition strategies for domains you are targeting in the aftermarket or drop market via our weekly watchlist.
Are bulk or portfolio purchases possible?
Yes. If you are interested in acquiring multiple domains, reach out directly to discuss portfolio pricing. Bulk acquisitions are handled case by case.
