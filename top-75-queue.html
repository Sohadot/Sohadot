What this queue is — and is not

Sohadot's Category Artifact Meaning Layer currently protects 50 domains with a canonical segmentation, a conceptual thesis, a category claim, and buyer logic — each reviewed, checked against misreadings, and locked before being shown to a buyer. Growing that set to 75 requires the same discipline, not a shortcut. This page is the first governed step: a public, transparent shortlist of 25 domains worth reviewing next, drawn from the 396 domains still pending Canonical Meaning Lock, selected for strategic category potential, exact-match clarity, fit with Sohadot's existing clusters, and low risk of turning out to be a generic weak brandable.

It is deliberately not a new meaning layer. No domain on this page has been added to data/asset-meanings.json or data/category-clusters.json. No candidate below is marked protected-category-artifact, and none has a finished, published canonical meaning. Each candidate record below is checked by scripts/validate_top_75_queue.py, which fails the build if a queued domain is already protected, is missing from the Conceptual Inventory Ledger, or is presented as anything more than a reviewed-later hypothesis.
