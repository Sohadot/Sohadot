Request a Strategic Brief

This channel is for buyers with a real acquisition question about a Sohadot-owned asset — a single category artifact, a strategic cluster, or a broader portfolio-level relationship. There is no price list here and no checkout. A brief request starts a direct, human conversation addressed to the specific asset, cluster, or thesis you name below.

What this is, and what it isn't

A Strategic Brief request is not a lead-generation form. It exists so a serious buyer can name exactly what they are evaluating — a single domain, a whole cluster, or a partnership shape — and receive a direct reply addressed to that, rather than a form-letter response.

  • No pricing table. Acquisition terms are discussed directly, per asset.
  • No "buy now" flow. This is the start of a conversation, not a checkout.
  • One recipient: agent@sohadot.com — no third-party CRM, no ad-platform retargeting pixel tied to this form.
  • Already have written agreement on terms? See the Seller-Approved Escrow Closing Protocol — independent escrow, not a public checkout.
  • Sohadot does not operate as a third-party domain broker — this channel handles inquiries on Sohadot-owned portfolio assets, not representation for domains Sohadot does not own.
