What This Page Is

This is not an automated dump of expiring names. It is a curated weekly shortlist built to surface domains that may justify investor attention based on structure, use-case potential, comparable sales context, and acquisition practicality.

How Sohadot Evaluates Weekly Drops
Structural quality: length, readability, memorability
Extension quality: .com, .ai, .io, .co favored
Category relevance: AI, tech, finance, health, geo, brandable
Comparable public sales used as directional reference
Trademark and obvious risk signals avoided where possible
Resale range shown as guidance, not guaranteed outcome

How to Use This List

1
Scan featured picks first. They are the highest-conviction names of the week based on current scoring and comparable context.
2
Use filters by category. AI, finance, geo, and health behave differently in the resale market.
3
Treat values as directional. A resale range is not a guarantee; buyer fit, timing, and competition matter.
4
Move early. Once a quality name drops, it can be registered by anyone at standard registration cost or captured elsewhere.
