What This Page Is

This is not a raw automated dump of expiring names. It is a curated weekly shortlist built to surface domains that may justify investor attention based on structure, category fit, naming quality, comparable-sales context, and practical acquisition logic.

How Sohadot Evaluates Weekly Drops
Structural quality: length, readability, memorability
Extension quality: .com, .ai, .io, .co prioritized
Category weighting: AI, tech, finance, health, geo, brandable
Public comparable-sales logic used as directional context
Trademark and obvious risk signals filtered where possible
Availability status framed carefully as watchlist guidance

How to Use This List

1
Start with featured picks. They represent the highest-conviction names for the current cycle based on quality, category, and market logic.
2
Filter by category. AI, finance, geo, health, and SaaS names behave differently in the aftermarket.
3
Treat values as directional. Resale ranges are guidance, not guarantees. Buyer fit, timing, and competition matter.
4
Use the status label carefully. Some names may be near-drop watchlist candidates rather than definitively available right now.
Important Note
This page is a curated research resource, not a promise of availability or resale outcome. Availability can change quickly. Status labels, comparable-sales logic, and estimated values are directional market signals only. Final acquisition results depend on timing, competing registrations, registrar processes, and actual end-user demand.
