Methodology
How This Model Thinks

This tool evaluates domains through a structured model that weighs lexical legitimacy, commercial keyword strength, TLD quality, naming structure, and comparable public sales context. It is designed to distinguish between commercially strong keywords, legitimate dictionary-based terms, personal-name domains, clean brandables, and weaker low-signal strings without relying on opaque black-box scoring alone.

What It Is Good For
Investors: Directional wholesale and retail framing.
Brokers: Faster triage for naming quality and market plausibility.
Founders: Early signal on naming credibility and extension fit.
Developers: Better context on lexical legitimacy and product suitability.
Important Note
This is a structured directional estimate, not a certified appraisal and not a guaranteed sale outcome.
Short premium assets, exact-match commercial terms, and buyer-specific opportunities can sell materially above any model output.
